Publishing Platform

Game Types

Indie/Surreal/RPG/Abstract/Round Fighting

Game Features

The features of the Hylics are advanced graphics/RPG battles/overuse of random text generation and about 2 hours of content.

More about Game

The imagination and the impact of art design mask some tiny shortcomings of the game.There are often conversations that you don't understand in it,because these paragraphs are generated by AI program.As a turn based fighting game,the player's maximum blood limit,regional transmission and some skills and props acquisition need to be completed after death.In addition, the character's skills need to be acquired by watching some strange TVs.Now if you are interested,you can purchase it directly in Steam.

What's our web for?

With the rapid development of video games, we often hear that games are the ninth art.This sentence is exactly the inspiration of my web and the origin of my web title.

Through this website, I hope to introduce those games that focus on artistic experience and make artistic aesthetics a critical element of games. By introducing these games, I hope to make more people realize that games are no less art forms than literature or movies, and let more people experience the charm of games.
